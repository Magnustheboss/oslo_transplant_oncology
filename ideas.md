## Selected Approach: **Scandinavian Modernism / Clinical Precision**

**Reasoning**:
This approach best aligns with the identity of the "Oslo" group—grounded, reliable, and world-leading. It balances the need for academic seriousness with the human element of patient care. The "Swiss Style" might be too stark for a medical group dealing with sensitive patient topics, and "Organic Futurism" might feel too experimental or "tech-startup" for a hospital-based research group. The Scandinavian Modernism approach offers a timeless, professional, and trustworthy aesthetic that honors the group's heritage while showcasing their innovation.

**Implementation Details**:
- **Font**: Manrope (Headings), Inter (Body)
- **Colors**: Nordic Blue (#0F4C81), White, Light Gray, Living Coral (#FF6F61) accent.
- **Visuals**: Clean, high-quality photos of the team (from Excalibur), abstract medical background patterns.
